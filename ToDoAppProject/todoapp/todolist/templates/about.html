ABOUT

ToDo App is simple and awesome app to organize your tasks with very easy to use interface. ToDo can help you to make list of your tasks and highlight the tasks which you haven't completed so that you can act according.

We believe that we only focus on our Today and Tomorrow tasks, which is most important. So we made user interface like that so that it can give you a quick view on your day timeline.

FUNCTIONS

  • Helps individuals in planning tasks according his/her needs and make them work efficiently

  • Alter your Task's Status / Priority / Title / Due date.

  • Saves your time by displaying highest priority task first.

  • Provides you some simple actions to modify tasks by reacting to emojis.

  • Login security is set to the highest standards.

  • No worries of losing one's tasks.

    • TODO app is busy tracking your activities while you are busy in the busy world.
